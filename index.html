Cherdly at our Yaletown location

Mane is a neighborhood barbershop located in Vancouver, BC. We offer traditional services and do them right. Book your appointment and come by to sip on some of the finest BC beer, kick back to a rotating selection of vinyl, shop from our curated clothing selection, and enjoy a tailored cut from one of our incredible barbers.

Services

Haircut

Skin Fade

Faded haircut from the skin of the scalp to desired length on top with hot lather and neck shave Starting at $40

Long Cut

Haircut starting below the base of the neck or chin, whether it is staying long or is being cut to a shorter length, and any transformational haircut Starting at $55

Bread Trim

Beard trim with scissor or clipper to shape and line up beard. Starting at $25
